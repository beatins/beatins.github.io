Disadvantages Retail Investors Face

Beatins is here to help all retail investors.

Information

Professional investors have research staffs that are constantly providing them with up-to-date information.

Diversification

As a retail investor, it's harder to build your own diversified portfolio. You don't have enough money to spread across various industries.

Costs

In the same way Walmart can negotiate a lower buying price than a family store, large investors are able to negotiate lower investment fees than retail investors.

Mood

There are all kinds of rumors over the internet. As a retail investor it's really hard to control the mood when making a significant decision.

Good investment is finding that perfect balance between return and risk and how it functions.
